About Me

Hello, I'm Mary Smith, a student of BYU Pathways and I'm a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints.

My favorite scripture passage is in the Book of Mormon, Alma 37:37 "Counsel with the Lord in all of thy doings and he will direct thee for good." Jesus Christ gave his life for us. He continues as the Living Christ to plan and direct our lives so that we can reach our potential. His Holy Spirit guides us be a help to those within our stewardship. Everyday we must counsel with him to make sure our plans align. When yoked with the Savior, we cannot fail!
